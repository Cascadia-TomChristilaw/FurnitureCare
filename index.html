Furniture Deluxe

Categories Info

Deluxing is the art of restoring a piece to pristine condition. This can include buffing out minor scratches, tightening loose parts, filling holes, performing stain or sealant touch-ups, cleaning, polishing, stain removal and any number of other tasks with the aim of restoring the beauty of the piece.
